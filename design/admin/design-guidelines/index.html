Minimal e-commerce system

One visual language for shop and admin.

Warm off-white background, white cards, black primary actions, soft borders, large radius, compact data tables, readable forms, custom controls, and responsive layouts for desktop, tablet, and phones.

WYSIWYG-safe content

Product story

Long content from CMS or WYSIWYG editors should scroll inside a readable block when needed and must not break the product buy panel.

  • Safe line height and spacing.
  • Long-word wrapping.
  • Scrollable content blocks.
  • Lists, links, headings, and tables.
